How it works?

We believe that buying or selling a home should not be full of stress and frustration.

  • Interest

    Buyers and sellers indicate the timeframe they’d like to purchase or sell a property, up to 2 years in advance.

  • Match

    Early Match connects the sellers and buyers when the time period, location and type of property match.

  • Deal

    If a deal is made, a 0.6% referral fee is applied to the seller during the conveyancing process.
